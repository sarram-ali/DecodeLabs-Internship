# 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as **Week 2** of my **Data Analytics Internship at Decode Labs**.

After cleaning the dataset in **Week 1**, this task focused on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, sales trends, payment preferences, referral channels, and overall dataset characteristics.

The analysis was performed using **Python** and **Pandas**, allowing meaningful business insights to be extracted from a real-world e-commerce dataset.

---

# 🎯 Internship Task

### Goal

Analyze the cleaned dataset to:

- Calculate descriptive statistics
- Identify trends and patterns
- Detect outliers
- Summarize important business observations

---

# 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **1,200 customer orders** with **14 columns** covering:

- Customer Information
- Products
- Quantity Purchased
- Unit Price
- Total Sales
- Payment Methods
- Order Status
- Coupon Codes
- Referral Sources
- Purchase Dates

The cleaned dataset from **Week 1** was used for this analysis.

---

# 🔍 Analysis Performed

## 📊 Descriptive Statistics

Calculated summary statistics including:

- Mean
- Median
- Minimum
- Maximum
- Standard Deviation

for numerical columns such as:

- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Items In Cart
- Total Price

---

## 📈 Product Performance Analysis

Analyzed:

- Total revenue generated by each product
- Total quantity sold for each product

### Key Findings

| Product | Revenue |
|---------|---------:|
| Chair | **$195,620** |
| Printer | **$195,613** |
| Laptop | **$192,127** |

Most purchased product:

- **Chair (562 units)**

---

## 💳 Payment Method Analysis

Analyzed customer payment preferences.

| Payment Method | Percentage |
|---------------|-----------:|
| Online | **21.5%** |
| Cash | **20.5%** |
| Credit Card | **19.5%** |
| Debit Card | **19.3%** |
| Gift Card | **19.2%** |

**Observation**

Customer payments are distributed fairly evenly across all payment methods.

---

## 📦 Order Status Analysis

Orders were grouped by their current status.

| Status | Orders |
|---------|-------:|
| Cancelled | 250 |
| Returned | 247 |
| Pending | 237 |
| Shipped | 235 |
| Delivered | 231 |

**Observation**

The dataset contains a balanced distribution across different order statuses.

---

## 🌐 Referral Source Analysis

Customer acquisition channels were analyzed.

| Source | Customers |
|---------|----------:|
| Instagram | 259 |
| Email | 250 |
| Google | 241 |
| Facebook | 228 |
| Referral | 222 |

**Observation**

Instagram generated the highest number of customer referrals.

---

## 📅 Monthly Sales Trend

Monthly sales revenue was calculated to identify seasonal patterns.

### Highest Revenue Month

- **June — approximately $170,616**

### Lowest Revenue Month

- **September — approximately $69,322**

---

## 🛒 Monthly Quantity Trend

Monthly product quantities sold were analyzed.

### Highest Quantity Sold

- **June — 458 products**

### Lowest Quantity Sold

- **September — 208 products**

This indicates that higher sales revenue closely aligned with higher product demand.

---

## 🚨 Outlier Detection

Outlier detection was performed using the **Interquartile Range (IQR)** method.

Analyzed column:

- Quantity

### Result

✅ No outliers were detected in the Quantity column.

---

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Chairs generated the highest overall revenue.
- Chairs were also the most frequently purchased product.
- June recorded the highest sales revenue and product demand.
- September experienced the lowest sales performance.
- Online payment was the most commonly used payment method.
- Instagram brought the highest number of customers.
- No unusual quantity values were found, indicating consistent purchasing behavior.
- Customer purchases were distributed relatively evenly across payment methods and order statuses.
